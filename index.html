Join Us in Making a Difference

SOPS (SOCIETY OF PROGRESSIVE STUDENTS) is an independent society founded in 2024. The words "Progressive" and "Students" draw inspiration from the renowned PDSU group established by George Reddy, a respected student leader and social activist, in 1972. His enduring values serve as a guiding force for students and society at large. SOPS, led by a team of devoted professionals from diverse backgrounds, is resolute in its commitment to sponsoring education for disadvantaged students and supporting marginalized and impoverished groups with essential needs such as food, shelter, and clothing.

Our Vision

Vision: We aim to provide aid and support to the most socioeconomically disadvantaged students, thereby granting them access to high-quality education and the ability to attain a dignified standard of living.

Our Mission

Mission: Impact 1 lakh people across different sections of society with quality education and dignified living by 2030.
